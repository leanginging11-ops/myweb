Program"Youth Camp on career Orientation"

On November 29 to December 1, 2025, the Cambodian Youth Network Association and Orida organised a “Youth Camp on Career Orientation” Program with a total of 25 participants, 13 women and 1 LGBTQ+ at the Vkirirom International School of Kampong Speu province.

Theme:Youth in Business Way Camp

rom October 17 to 19, 2025, the Cambodian Youth Network Association (CYN) led a group to participated camp theme: "Youth in Business Way Camp".This event was a collaboration between CYN and OREDA, hosting a total of 11 participants, including 7 women and one monk from by CYN. Empowering Youth At Asean people's Forum 2025

Empowering Youth at ASEAN People's Forum 2025! Cambodia Youth Network Association (CYN) is pleased to share our successful engagement at the ASEAN Civil Society Conference/ ASEAN People’s Forum (ACSC/APF) 2025 in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, from October 13 to 16, 2025! We actively supported the forum’s mission to amplify grassroots voices and strengthen cross-border solidarity.
